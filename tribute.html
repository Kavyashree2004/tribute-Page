Kuvempu

About Kuvempu

Kuppali Venkatappa Puttappa, popularly known as Kuvempu, was a towering figure in Kannada literature. He was the first Kannada writer to receive the Jnanpith Award and is widely regarded as one of the greatest poets of the 20th century.

He advocated for "Vishwamanava" — the universal man — and his works are known for their depth, spirituality, and strong message of unity. His contributions have left a lasting legacy in Indian literature and culture.
